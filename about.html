About the Moabite Federation

Who We Are

The Moabite Federation is a sovereign Indigenous nation dedicated to restoring the rights, land, and spiritual identity of the original people of the Americas. We honor our ancestral ties, governed under divine law, tribal sovereignty, and community-driven principles.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to reclaim our divine birthright, preserve our culture, and build generational wealth through decentralized tools such as the Sovran Wealth Fund (SWF), land stewardship, and educational empowerment.

Why It Matters

As descendants of ancient Moors, Washitaw, and Yamassee lineages, we have been historically misclassified and dispossessed. The Moabite Federation serves as a beacon for those seeking to restore their true status, land rights, and spiritual inheritance.

Join Us

We invite all who resonate with this purpose to unite, learn, and build together. Membership is open to all who commit to truth, law, and legacy.
